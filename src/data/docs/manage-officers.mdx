# Officer Management Guide
This guide outlines the steps to manage officers within the ORC.GOV portal from the dashboard.

## Accessing the Officers Page
- Log in to your ORC.GOV dashboard.

- Click on the Application Management tab in the sidebar.

![user-management screenshot](/admin/user1.png)

- Select the Officers card.


![user-management screenshot](/admin/officer1.png)


This action navigates you to the Officers page, where a table displays all currently listed officers.

- click the New button.


![user-management screenshot](/admin/officer2.png)

A New Officer Form modal will appear.

- Select the appropriate Staff from the dropdown list.

- Click the Submit button to complete the process.

![user-management screenshot](/admin/officer3.png)
